About Me

Hello ! I'm Matt Loera, a senior Computer Science student at PSU. I'm passionate about exploring the intersection of technology and social justice, and have experience in both Web Design and Systems Programming. My particular interest lies in issues related to data sovereignty, data justice, and AI ethics. I strongly believe that technology has the potential to create positive change in the world, but only if it's used responsibly and ethically.

I'm deeply committed to using my skills and knowledge to ensure that technology is harnessed to create a more equitable and just society. This means delving deep into the ethical and legal implications of data collection, storage, and usage, and exploring ways in which technology can be used to protect individual rights and promote social justice.

Outside of my studies, I'm an avid biker and a proud member of the biking community in Portland. I love the sense of community and connectedness that biking brings, and it helps me stay grounded and connected with the world around me.

Overall, my goal is to use my passion for Computer Science and my commitment to social justice to create innovative solutions that make a positive impact on the world.
